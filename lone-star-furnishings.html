Texas Roots. National Reach.
Lone Star Furnishings was founded in 2001 by a former district operations team that believed Texas schools deserved bid-compliant furniture support from a local partner who speaks procurement language. We built our process around TXMAS, cooperative contracts, and dependable delivery windows aligned with instructional calendars. Today we serve K-12 districts, charter networks, colleges, and municipalities with a dedicated planning team, certified installers, and project managers trained on educational occupancy timelines.
Our HUB certification and supplier network help districts satisfy participation goals while still maintaining strict spec controls. We coordinate freight staging, inside delivery, punch-list closeout, and warranty documentation so campus leaders can focus on teaching—not logistics.
